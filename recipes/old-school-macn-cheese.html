Old School Mac'n Cheese

Decription

There is nothing quite like grandma's cooking. We may not be able to duplicate it, but we can get close with this recipe.

A nice white sauce with some cheese and a few other flavors with macaroni and a potato chip crust

Ingredients

  1. 1 ¾ pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  2. ¾ cup butter
  3. ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  4. 6 cups milk, divided
  5. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  6. 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  7. 1 teaspoon onion powder
  8. 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  9. salt and ground black pepper to taste
  10. 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  11. 3 (8 ounce) packages shredded American cheese
  12. 1 (8 ounce) bag potato chips (such as Lay's®), crushed
  13. 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  14. ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  15. butter-flavored cooking spray
